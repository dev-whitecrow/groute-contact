1. Automatic collection of information

When you use the Mobile Application, our servers automatically record information that your device sends. This data may include information such as your device's IP address and location, device name and version, operating system type and version, language preferences, information you search for in the Mobile Application, access times and dates, and other statistics.

  • Camera and Photo Data: When you use the in-app camera feature, we access your device's camera to capture photos for travel documentation purposes. These photos are processed locally on your device and may be stored securely in cloud storage services (Firebase) operated by Google for backup, synchronization, and account recovery purposes.
  • Location Information: We collect precise location data from photo metadata (EXIF data) when you capture or import photos. This location information is used exclusively to automatically map your travel journey and create location-based travel records on an interactive map powered by Google Maps.
  • Photo Gallery Access: When you choose to import existing photos from your device gallery, we access only the photos you specifically select for your travel documentation.

Information collected automatically is used only to identify potential cases of abuse and establish statistical information regarding the usage of the Mobile Application and Services. This statistical information is not otherwise aggregated in such a way that would identify any particular User of the system.
